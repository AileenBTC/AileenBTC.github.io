      About BTS:

    BTS known as Bangtan boys is a 7 member South Korean kpop group from South Korea formed by bighit entertainment. They debut with there song "No More Dream" from there album 2 cool 4 skool on June 13, 2013. Later on the years they began a series called Most Beautiful Moments in Life where each album tells a different stories and life lessons about youth and that we should enjoy it with the time we have. This series has been one of the most anticipated albums in Korea spreading throughout the world and even ending on the billboad charts. This group is also known for there dancing leaving there fans screaming for more with the intense choreography

      Wings
